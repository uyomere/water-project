BACKGROUND

Background: we would like to share how we got here, since 2010 we have walked the part of humanitarian works in areas such as: purpose discovery, education, climate action, health support for children and adolescents, child protection, monitoring, water, conflict resolution and partnership, under names such as New Age Youths Organization (NAYO), and New Age Foundation (NAF), and New Age Organization (NAO), but in 2020, we had a new name; The Green Child International Organization (www.thegreenchild.org) Over the years people who have seen our work will agree that we have done so much, while this is true, especially for an organization that has not received funding from any local or foreign donor organization, yet against all odds The Green Child International has thrived to keep actualization the SDGs, thereby helping humanity and also improving social conditions. Having seen so many limitations especially on areas of inadequate fund and lack of training for our staff, the founder of The Green Child International Organization made a move by establishing a Bottling Water Company (CAP), a company which profits will be channeled to humanitarian works

ABOUT US

Captop table water was founded in 2023 by Alagba Bakosi Chinedu, however he was on the drawing board for three years planning on the sustainability and the right time to start, with the vision to give millions of Africans access to one of the clean water they have ever drank. Cap water is not only about satisfying human thirst, its more about saving lives, profit made from the company will be channeled to humanitarian works, which is one of the motivation behind the establishment of the company. The company’s profit will be used to fund: access to clean water in communities, fund institution and organization that are into climate action and to make education accessible in rural communities and fund technological innovative ideas.

MISSION

Our mission is to solve water related challenges, promote education and drastically reduce climate change in our universe, through company’s profit.

VISION

Making water accessible to all
